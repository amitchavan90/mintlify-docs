# Widgets for Seamless AI Integration

Widgets are essential tools for seamlessly integrating your AI agent into various touchpoints across your website or application. By creating and customizing widgets, you can extend the functionality of your AI agent, ensuring it’s accessible to customers for tasks like support, inquiries, or bookings.

### Accessing Widget Settings

1. **Go to Settings**
    - Log in to your OneInbox.ai account.
    - On your dashboard, navigate to the **Settings** section.

2. **Click on Widget**
    - In the settings menu, find and click on **Widget**. This is where you can manage and create widgets for your AI agent.

### Creating a New Widget

1. **Click on Add Widget**
    - Click on the **Add Widget** button. A pop-up will appear to guide you through the widget creation process.

2. **Enter the Widget Name**
    - In the pop-up, you'll see a field to enter the name of your widget.
    - Type in a descriptive and memorable name for your widget like “Customer Support” or a custom name.

3. **Create the Widget**
    - Once you’ve entered the name, click on **Create Widget**.

1. **Widget Created!**
    - And that’s it! Your widget is now created and ready to be customized and used. You can configure its settings further to tailor it to your specific needs.
